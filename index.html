Dastaan-e-Yaafai

Zindagi ka safar hi bahut kar chuke
Ghamo se aahein bahut bhar chuke
Bahut khaye dhoke ab seekhne ki baari
Kya hai hamara haal, ab likhne ki baari
Unhe khaas bataate bataate hum aam hogaye,
Un se ishq karke hum badnaam hogaye.
Adaa matlab, nigaah matlab, zabaa matlab, bayaa matlab —
binaa matlab kaha jaau? Jahaa jaau waha matlab.
Kitna istemaal kiye gaye hum ishq me koi puchte to hisaab likhdete 😗
Wo mangti agar humse mohabbat ki safai to puri kitaab likhdete 😍🙌
Kisi se shiqwa🙂
Kisi se maqdood rehta hoon 😌
Mohabbat naam ki bimaari se mehfooz rehta hoon 😎
Najaane kyun ye ankhen aaj nam hai
Aur zindagi me masroof aaj bahot hum hai
Dhundete rehte hai humesha zindagi ke samandar me khushiyon ku
Par humare samandar me to sirf gam hi gam hai
Sari umr gaalib wo jhoote sapne dikhate rahe
The kuch na humare wo par Hume apna pan jataate rahe
Hume socha wo to acche hai
Dil se to wo humare liye sacche hai
Samne hasate rahe peeche rulaate rahe
Hum to dil se chahte the unku per wo puri zindagi hum ko sataate rahe
Hum to gairon ko samajhte the astin ka saanp gaalib
Par Hume to dasa usi ne jise Hum apna bataate rahe
Bohot sabr kiya hai maine bhi pyaar me
Zulfe safed hogayi tere intezaar me
Tum bhi fahad zulm beintehaayi karte ho
Wafa ki aas dikha kar bewafaai karte ho
Tum bhi gaalib kamaal karte ho bewafa se wafa ka sawaal karte ho
Tum ne humaari zindagi ku kuch yu sawaara hai
Ke meri gamgeen💥 zindagi ku khusyion ke khoshboo se bahara hai 🗿
Sab matlabi hai bas tu hi jahan humara hai 🙌🏼
Har lamhe me is dil ne tujhe pukaara hai🥺
Aur wo puchte wafa ka saboot ham se gaalib
Are dil cheer ke dekho mera isme naam sirf tumhara hai 🫣
Baat kuch na thi gaalib fir bhi wo bahaane banaane lage hai 😔
Gairon se zyaada aaj kal apne rulaane lage hai 🥺
Koile ke jalne se hire ko kya farak padta hai 😔
Hamare sonchne na sonche ne se kisiko kya farakh padhta hai 😪
Log puch te hai mujhe ke ke mai tere pyaar me kyun padhta hoon 😑
Une kya pata ke me tujhse saccha pyar karta hoon 💗
Bas tanhai me tujhe khodene ke dar se dar ta hoon 💥
Log puchte ke tujh me aisa kya hai 💗
Maine bhi kaha ke jab dil sacche hai ishq me to fir ye paisa kya hai 😃
Tere liye to jaan har din me sajta sawar ta hoon 😘
Log dhoondte rehgaye teri khoobsoorti nakaab me
Unhe kya pata ke me teri adaaon pe marta hoon
Ae raat tere bin ye sapne dekhe nhi jaate 😔
Aap 4 din ki baat karti ho are naye Rishtao me din dekhe nhi jaate 🥺🥺
Gamgheen haalat me bhi ab sabar karna padega 😔
Aap ke bagair akele raaston pe safar karna padega 🥺
Apnaon ke ghar hone par kisi gair ke darwaze khatakta nhi 😔
Itna samjha tha hoon ise par ye dil-e-naadan samajh ta nahi 😪
Is khatarnaak sher ka bas tu hi ek chaara hai 😚
Tere ishq me ye maashooq kuwaara hai 😔
Teri yaadon me dil ye bhatak ta bechaara hai 😴
Teri yaadon se dil ye dhadkta bechaara hai 🫣
Are doctor dhundte rehge bimaari galib 😶‍🌫️
Unhe kya pata Hume to teri kaatil nigaaho ne maara hai 👀
